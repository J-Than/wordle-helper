WORDLE Assistant

Instructions:

1. Type in the letters of your word.

2. Press "Update" (or "Enter") at any time to confirm letters and display possible matches.

3. Change colors by tapping the corresponding letter key or clicking the box.

Note: Adding a new word will lock all previous words from further changes.

The default word list is from WORDLE offical site. Some apps may use different lists. Use the top center button to switch to a larger list.
